Audience and Goals

After completing the primary and secondary research, I looked towards my audience. Who would be viewing this website? Who would be using this website? I then looked at certain goals that applied to my audience.

Audience

I began by listing the people who were involved with my client as well as the skeptics/critics of ARF.

This was the result of my first attempt at defining my audience. I came up with three main groups, the donors, volunteers, and people looking to adopt. After that, I began brainstorming members of each of these broad groups. I then turned to the skeptics, and listed those.

After determining members of each of the broad groups, I began to see that many of them contained the same members. Some members fit every group, such as "animal lovers." I then rearranged my cards to include a "fits into all three" and to show which members fit into more than one, but not all.

I then narrowed my audience down further by grouping together all families and no longer distinguishing between families with children, etc. This gave me my main audience, adults, college students, animal lovers, families, and high school students.

Define Goals

In this section, I defined my overarching goals as well as my specific goals for specific user groups.

Overarching Goals

Client
  • More adoptions happen.
  • More volunteers coming to help.
  • More monetary donations.
  • Spread their message and who they are.
  • More physical donations. (cleaning supplies, cat litter, etc.)
  • More widespread recognition.
  • Connect with other shelters and animal organizations.
Audience
  • Adopt an animal.
  • Find out who ARF is and what they do.
  • Volunteer to help.
  • Give monetary donations.
  • Find out what physical donations are being accepted and how to donate them.
  • Find resources on being a good pet owner.
Website
  • Make information easy to find.
  • Increase money and physical donations.
  • Have current information on which animals are available to adopt.
  • Make it easy to fill out adoption forms online.
  • Easy access to information regarding being a good pet owner.
  • Connect with other shelters and animal organizations.

Specific Goals

A lot of these goals are the same for various user groups, as most of them fit into most of the main groups (volunteers, donors, people looking to adopt an animal.)
